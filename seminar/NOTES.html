Log normal prior

Motivation : 0 becomes unlikely as soon as there is something. 0 is highly likely when looking at the whole surface of earth.

Will have a mode in the relatively small values, but will also leave room for extreme values -> high concentration of productivity, say, in urban clusters. This behaviour is well described by fat tailed distributions, such as log-normal.
